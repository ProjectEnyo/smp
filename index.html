You are safe from PvP in the spawn area, but you may build there. Leaving it for the Wilderness means you can be killed by other players.

You are responsible for your own builds when it comes to griefing. To avoid getting your base griefed, you can protect it by claiming the blocks around it. As a new player, you may create a small claim centered around you with /claim. Future claims requires a golden shovel. Remember that players can enter your claim if they have an open way inside.
